Tilaka is a limited liability company that already has an Electronic Certification Operator (“PSrE”) license as per Recognition Decree No. 423 of 2021 with Parent Recognition Status issued by the Ministry of Communication and Information.
By continuing to use the Mekari e-Sign services, you agree and authorize us to store, share forward your information and/or data related to the use of the Mekari e-Sign service to Tilaka for the purpose of providing the Mekari e-Sign service.         1        Name according to ID card
        2        E-mail
        3        Phone number
        4        ID card photo
        5        Selfie
        6        Electronic document to be affixed with PSrE Electronic Certificate.
Purpose of providing Data:
        1        Issue, revoke, and/or re-issue Owner's Certificates

        3        Collect, record, and analyze data related to the activity of using the Tilaka Electronic Signature Service

        5        Send information by email regarding the failure to protect your Personal Data
        6        Send you instructions regarding the compromise or misuse of the Owner's Certificate
        7        Publish the Certificate Revocation List (CRL)
        8        Send information on changes to Public Documents and
        9        Use or disclose your Personal Data in order to assist law enforcement based on orders from the court and/or from Law Enforcement Officials in accordance with the provisions of the applicable laws and regulations.
